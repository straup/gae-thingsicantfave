Things I Can't Fave

This is a Very Simple™ web application that allows you to favourite things you can't otherwise fave on Flickr. Things like sets, collections, galleries and comments.

How does it work?

  • You can fave galleries, sets, collections, groups, people and comments on photos.
  • You can't comments on galleries. Yet.
  • You can't fave your own stuff.

How do I add a fave?

  • You fave something by copying its (Flickr) URL in to the form on the front page.
  • You can also drag the fave this bookmarklet to your browser's toolbar to fave stuff directly from Flickr!

How do I find out about new stuff?

  • There is a thingsicantfave Twitter account that gets updated every time a new fave is added
  • There are RSS feeds at the bottom of all the pages. You can subscribe to a list of all the faves or just those for a particular user.

Can I get my faves out of this thing?

  • You can export a list of all your faves at any time.
